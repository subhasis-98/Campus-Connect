📝 Campus Wall – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Share announcements or casual updates with students.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. 📂 Navigate to the Campus Wall tab.
    2. ✍️ Enter post title and content.
    3. 📤 Click “Post” to publish.
    4. 📋 View it live in the feed below.
  • 👥 Who Can Use: All logged-in students.
  • 🗂️ Optional: Filter posts by category or department.

📅 Events – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Share upcoming campus events or fests.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. ➕ Click “Create Event”.
    2. 📝 Add title, date, venue, and description.
    3. ✅ Submit the form.
    4. 🙋 Others can RSVP or show interest.
  • 👨‍💼 Who Can Use: Faculty, coordinators, or club heads.
  • ⏳ Optional: Add countdown to each event.

📚 Notes Share – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Upload or download class notes and resources.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. 📁 Go to Notes Share section.
    2. 🔗 Upload file or add Google Drive link.
    3. 🏷️ Tag it with subject/semester.
    4. 📤 Click “Upload”.
  • 👥 Who Can Use: All students.
  • 🔎 Optional: Filter by subject or title.

🗳️ Polls & Surveys – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Collect opinions through quick polls.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. ➕ Click “Create Poll”.
    2. ❓ Enter question & answer choices.
    3. 📤 Click “Publish”.
    4. 📊 Users vote and see live chart results.
  • 👨‍🏫 Who Can Use: Admins or faculty.
  • 👤 Optional: Show creator’s name & DP.

🔔 Noticeboard – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Share important college-wide announcements.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. 📢 Click “Add Notice”.
    2. 📝 Add title and description.
    3. 🚨 Mark as “Urgent” if needed.
    4. 📤 Post the notice.
  • 👨‍🏫 Who Can Use: Admin or faculty only.
  • 📌 Optional: Pin notices at the top.

🧾 User Profile – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Display user's profile info.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. 👤 Open Profile tab.
    2. ✍️ Fill out your info and upload a DP.
    3. 💾 Save your profile.
    4. 👀 Preview your profile layout.
  • 👥 Who Can Use: Any user (static view).
  • 🧠 Optional: Show recent posts or activity.

💬 CollegeQ – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Post questions and answer your peers.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. ❓ Click “Ask Question”.
    2. 🏷️ Add title, content, and tags.
    3. 📤 Submit to post.
    4. 👍 Users can upvote and reply.
  • 👥 Who Can Use: Any student.
  • 🔄 Optional: Sort by latest or most upvoted.

👥 Student Clubs – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Join and follow student clubs.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. 📂 Browse available clubs.
    2. 👀 View club info.
    3. 📌 Click “Join” to follow.
  • 👩‍🎓 Who Can Use: All students.
  • 📅 Optional: Show upcoming club events.

🛍️ Marketplace – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Buy and sell used campus items.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. ➕ Click “List Item”.
    2. 📷 Upload image, set price & description.
    3. 📤 Post your listing.
    4. 📞 Buyers can contact you directly.
  • 👨‍🎓 Who Can Use: All students.
  • ✅ Optional: Mark as “Sold”.

🔍 Lost & Found – How to Use

  • 🎯 Purpose: Report and search for lost/found items.
  • 🛠️ Steps:
    1. ➕ Click “Report Lost” or “Report Found”.
    2. 📝 Add item name, details & location.
    3. 📸 (Optional) Upload a photo.
    4. 📤 Submit to share.
  • 👥 Who Can Use: Students and staff.
  • ✅ Optional: Mark item as “Claimed”.
